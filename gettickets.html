Read before booking tickets:

  • Besides Regular Entry tickets, there are Group Entry tickets which you can use to register 7 or more attendees, and Early Bird tickets which are available only to the first 10 registrants.
  • No TEDx event is complete without a souvenir to remember it by. So along with any ticket, for Rs 180 you can also get a gift hamper delivered to your address.
    The hamper's contents are specially designed for the event, are completely eco-friendly and include a bamboo sipper, notebook, seed pens and pencils, and cookies.
  • The event will be held online and to access it you'll need a google account. Make sure you use your gmail ID when prompted to fill in your Email.
  • Screen sharing during the event is not allowed. Doing so will get oneself banned from the event without refund.
